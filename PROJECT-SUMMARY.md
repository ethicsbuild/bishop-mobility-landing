## 🎯 Design Philosophy

The landing page was built with three core principles:

### 1. Dignity First
- No condescending language or imagery
- Respects the user like the product respects its wearer
- Professional design that doesn't infantilize
- Engineered like a tool, not a medical prop

### 2. Accessibility as Standard
- Large, readable fonts (not just "bigger")
- High contrast for visibility challenges
- Simple navigation (no complex menus)
- Mobile-first for tablet users
- Clear calls-to-action
- Form fields with proper labels

### 3. Storytelling with Purpose
- Jim's personal journey creates authenticity
- Problem → Solution → Proof narrative arc
- Technical details for those who care
- Emotional connection without manipulation
- Trust indicators throughout

The page is designed to maximize lead capture:

### Multiple Conversion Paths
1. Hero CTA button → Lead form
2. "Our Story" button → Founder section → Lead form

- Clear privacy statement

- Early bird pricing incentive

## 🎨 Visual Design

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Blue (#2563eb) - Trust, reliability, medical
- **Accent**: Green checkmarks - Success, approval
- **Background**: White with subtle blue gradients
- **Text**: Dark gray (#111827) for readability

### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, large (48-72px on desktop)
- **Body**: 18-20px base size
- **Buttons**: 18px with generous padding
- **Line height**: 1.6-1.8 for easy reading

### Layout
- **Max width**: 1280px (comfortable reading)
- **Spacing**: Generous padding and margins
- **Grid**: 2-column on desktop, single column on mobile
- **Cards**: Subtle shadows, rounded corners
- **Sections**: Clear visual separation
